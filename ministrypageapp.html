Prayer is more than just asking for things; it should also include worship,confession, and thanksgiving. We need to be specific about the people and things which we pray for.

Sunday:

Thank God that Jesus Christ rose frome the dead; pray for all members of youth department in your church and through out the world.

Monday:

Thank God for His love and care for us and our families; pray for faithful witness to those members of our families who are not Christians.

Tuesday:

Praise God for the grace that made you a member of the Church of Christ; pray for all leaders

Wednesday:

Praise God that He is our Creator and King; pray for the rulers of the nations,especially of your own nation.

Thursday:

Thank God for the suffering and death of Jesus, as a result of which we can live;pray for those who are suffering as a result of war,or their faith in Christ.

Friday:

Thank God for those who write and publish Christian literature, including the Daily Power:pray for the eyes of many to be opened through reading the Bible

Saturday:

Thank God for our places of work and study and all we have learnt and been able to do this week;commit it to Him the work and service of the coming week.
